About Us

The Paizo Chamber Ensemble is a student-led music community, dedicated to performing and promoting chamber music. In addition to performances, Paizo members tutor younger musicians, helping them improve their skills and confidence. Through collaboration, mentorship, and artistry, Paizo inspires audiences and nurtures a lasting love for classical music within the community.
