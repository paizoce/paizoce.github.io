Get Tutoring

Our Free Tutoring Program

Whether you’re looking to strengthen your musical skills or gain confidence in your performances, Paizo’s free tutoring program connects you with experienced student musicians who can guide you every step of the way.

Why Get Tutoring?

  • Learn from experienced peers: Receive guidance from talented student musicians who have excelled in your instrument or field.
  • Improve your technique and confidence: Get one-on-one feedback to refine your playing and perform with greater assurance.
  • Completely free of cost: Paizo’s tutoring sessions are entirely free for all students—no registration fees or hidden costs.
  • Convenient and nearby: Tutoring will be held at the closest Paizo campus to you for easy access.
  • Join a supportive community: Learn in an encouraging environment where growth, creativity, and collaboration come first.
