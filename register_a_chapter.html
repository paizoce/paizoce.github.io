How to Register a Chapter

Starting a new Paizo Chapter is an exciting way to bring the joy of chamber music to your local community. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Gather your team: Bring together motivated musicians and supporters at your school who share Paizo’s vision of musical collaboration.
  • Designate leadership: Choose a Chapter Head and at least three founding members to help guide your chapter’s activities.
  • Plan your events: Be prepared to host or participate in at least two musical events per semester, such as recitals, workshops, or community performances.
  • Align with Paizo’s mission: Ensure your chapter upholds our goal of using music to connect and inspire communities.
